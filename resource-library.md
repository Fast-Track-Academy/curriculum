## 🎯 Tool Selection Guidelines

### Before Choosing Any Tool, Ask:
1. **Does this solve a specific problem I have?**
2. **Is there a free alternative that works for my needs?**
3. **Will I actually use this regularly?**
4. **Does it integrate with my existing tools?**
5. **Can I afford the long-term cost?**

### Free vs. Paid Decision Matrix:
- **Choose Free When**: Learning the basics, experimenting, personal projects
- **Upgrade to Paid When**: Hitting usage limits, need premium features, professional use
- **Avoid Paid When**: Haven't fully utilized the free tier, multiple similar tools

---
